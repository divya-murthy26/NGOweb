Welcome to FCI

Empowering Women in Tech

FCI is a transformative NGO dedicated to empowering young women with industry-ready skills in technology. Through technical training, soft skills development, and mentorship, we bridge the gap between education and employment. Our ‘Future Leaders’ emerge as skilled, confident professionals, ready to break barriers and shape a more inclusive tech industry.Join us in building a future where every girl has the opportunity to lead, innovate, and thrive!
