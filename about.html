About Us

At Fund a Child in India (FCI), we believe in empowering young women with the skills and confidence needed to thrive in the tech industry. FCI is a dedicated NGO that focuses on building a strong foundation for girls in technology.

Our primary mission is to equip UVCE girls with essential industry-ready skills, helping them bridge the gap between education and employment. Through technical training, soft skills development, and aptitude coaching, we prepare them for a bright future in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
